Flightmare

Mystery Class

First appearance:

All appearances:

Behaviour and personality:

Flightmares are solitary dragons, meaning that they are the only defence for their food. Because of this, they are relentless when defending their food sources from perceived threats.

They prefer to remain airborne and can thus fly great distances with little rest. They are built for speed and defence, which means that they avoid fights with larger and stronger dragons if they can.

Trivia:

Flightmares' algae eating behaviour is similar to that of Flamingos, minus the aggression. Allegedly.

Only dragon to have been put in more than three dragon classes, namely Fear, Strike, Mystery, and Unknown.

Design resembles a cross between the Skrill and Fireworms.

Appears to be based on a pelican eel, a viper fish, a moon jellyfish, and a banshee.

One of two dragons that glows naturally and via eating something. The other is a Fireworm.
