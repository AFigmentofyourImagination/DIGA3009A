Dramillion

< × >

Mystery Class

First appearance:

All appearances:

Behaviour and personality:

These dragons are loyal to fault and will protect each other at any and all cost. They are, after all, social dragons. That said, they do not like to fight and will only do so if extremely necessary.

Features:

  • Spiky head
  • Small forelimbs
  • Finned tail

Abilities:

  • Mimic fire from other dragons
  • Sharing fire reserves
  • Camouflage (Titan Wings only)

Diet:

Berries

Habitat:

Unknown / Never mentioned

Size:

6,1 m (20 ft)

Wingspan:

10,67 m (35 ft) [unrealistic/DreamWorks official measurement]

6,58 m (21,583 ft) [realistic/based on model sheet]

Weight:

272,2 kg (600 lbs)

Fire Type:

Flaming facsimiles

Weaknesses:

  • Flight (group) dependency
  • (Titanwing) Enhanced physical abilities
  • (Titanwing) Immune to Dragon Root arrows
  • (Titanwing) Produces unique type of fire type made of yellow and purple flames that explodes on impact.
  • (Titanwing) Camouflage, like Changewings

Trivia:

  • When a Dramillion runs out of shots, the rest of the flight will share their reserves by transferring their flames to the spent Dramillion.
  • Distant relatives to the Changewings.
  • Can sleep hanging upside-down by their tails like a bat.
  • Some calls resemble the coos of a pigeon.
  • Second highest shot count.
