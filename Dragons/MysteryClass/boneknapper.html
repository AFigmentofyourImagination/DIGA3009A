Behaviour and personality:

Boneknappers spend a lot of time tediously searching through bones for the best/perfect pieces for their armour. Some even spend years, decades, searching and perfecting their armour.

In the special, Legend of the Boneknapper Dragon, it is demonstrated that once the dragon has completed their armour, they become very calm and friendly towards humans. Once they receive affection, they exhibit more puppy-like behaviour.

Trivia:

  • Concept art by Nico Marlet shows that the Boneknapper's design went through several revisions. Some of these designs had extra skulls on their bodies, two pairs of wings, numerous tails, and armour made of sharp weapons.
  • Dragons are most likely members of the Archosauria clade, which includes dinosaurs and crocodiles. The skull worn as a helmet by the Boneknapper appears to have archosaur fenestrae (skull holes). It has mandibular fenestrae and antorbital fenestrae that are closed.
  • According to Richard Hamilton, staff writer for the Netflix show 'Race to the Edge', the Boneknapper was removed from the franchise's canon. He revealed that the Boneknapper was created outside of How to Train Your Dragon and was added to the film very late in production. He also stated that the creative team behind Legend of the Boneknapper Dragon made decisions that did not align with the franchise's direction in How to Train Your Dragon 2 and beyond.
