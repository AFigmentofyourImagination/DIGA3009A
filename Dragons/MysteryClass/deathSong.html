Death Song

Mystery Class

First appearance:

All appearances:

Behaviour and personality:

Babies can also mimic sounds and songs. And they are noisy too, only soothed by song or food. I suppose that is the same as human babies.

They use their amber to make their nests. They don't eat the bones of their prey, preferring to stack them in a giant pile. I, the writer, speculate they use some bones in their nest.

Death Songs are notoriously solitary dragons, thus abandoning their own hatchlings and displaying hostility towards them, unless the hatchling is able to sing a song that resonates with them.

Trivia:

When they call, an extra vertebrae expands into frills and allows the song to project up to a mile away.

It appears that if another dragon or animal closely bonds with a Death Song, they can become immune to the song.

Slitherwing venom prevents the amber from solidifying upon contact.

Named by the character Tuffnut.

They grow very fast, in less than a year, they grow from hatchling to grown adult.
