Slitherwing

First appearance:

All appearances:

Behaviour and personality:

Slitherwings are territorial dragons, known for aggressive behaviour and tendency for hunting in a group. They use their toxins to subdue their prey (other dragons) and transport them to their expansive underground dwellings for the whole flight to eat.

Trivia:

Possible that they are the natural enemies of Death Songs.

Despite their appearance, they are not snakes. (Snakes have forked tongues, and Slitherwings have teeth all over their upper and lower jaws.)

Slightly resembles Gigantophis or Titanoboa.

Slitherwing's boxy head bears a striking resemblance to an oriental dragon. Its body is patterned like a San Francisco garter snake and coloured like a coral snake.

Due to its legless body and two wings, the Slitherwing is technically considered a form of Amphiptere, a legless dragon with two wings and a serpentine body. It shares this similarity with the Whispering Death and Screaming Death.

Due to how quick the venom affected Astrid after punching one (see "Race to the Edge", Season 5, Episode 10), and collapsing after catching up to the others, Slitherwing venom appears to be neurotoxic. Elapids like cobras, kraits, mambas, sea snakes, taipans, Australian brown snakes, Death Adders, and coral snakes have neurotoxic venom. In theory, it could be many times more potent than the venom of an Inland Taipan, or even a Black Mamba, combined.

Like Coral Snakes, the Slitherwing's bright coloration acts as a warning for other animals not to get too close.
