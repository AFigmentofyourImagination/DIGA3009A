Behaviour and personality:

Changewings nest in large, tall forests with their flights. because they are very maternal dragons, they will protect their young at all costs. This leads to the effect that these dragons are team players and will share a common goal in their flight, especially when united against threats. It is reminiscent to how a pride of lions work.

They are curious creatures that love to copy whatever they see. They are nocturnal dragons since it is easier to hide in the dark.

Changewings periodically shed their skin in fairly uniform strips, and from this we can see their skin is very thick despite it being softer. Shed skin still maintains their camouflage ability, though the specifics are never discussed and only shown once in the entire franchise.

Trivia:

Early concept art had the Changewing as a much slender snake-like dragon, with smaller wings and no horns.

Since the acid they spew can stay intact underwater, it may indicate it is oil based.

Since the acid can melt trees, but not rocks or metal, aside from Dragon Proof Metal, it suggests the acid melts mostly biological matter.

Their ability to stay upside down leads to speculation that they have adherent feet like geckos.

Eggs have gem-like qualities and is green in base, but glows multiple colours. Can be mistaken by Vikings as "stones of good fortune".

It is documented that Changewings can raise Vikings as their own babies, though it has only been seen once when a very maternal Changewing captured one of the main characters, Snotlout, and cuddled and played with him until intervention by Hiccup.

Changewings and Dramillions are distantly related species.

Their facial appearance is similar to Diplocaulus's, an extinct prehistoric amphibian from the Permian period. More similar to an octopus than chameleon.

Chameleons change colour due to mood, and octopi change colour and texture to camouflage themselves.
