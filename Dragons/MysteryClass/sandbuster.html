Sandbuster

× >

Mystery Class

First appearance:

Race to the Edge, Season 5, Episode 2 "Sandbusted"

All appearances:

Race to the Edge, Season 5, Episode 2 "Sandbusted"

Race to the Edge, Season 6, Episode 1 "In Plain Sight"

Behaviour and personality:

Described as territorial and greedy, they protect their treasure like pirates, though they are just aggressive in general.

Features:

  • Large head
  • Long neck
  • Prehensile tail
  • Spikes alongside of body

Abilities:

  • Burrowing
  • Prehensile Tail
  • Fire Ricochet on Impact
  • Shoots superheated sand that hardens to solid glass on impact. Glass is opaque.

Diet:

Unknown / Never mentioned

Habitat:

Caverns

Beaches

Size:

24,38 m (80 ft)

Wingspan:

15,24 m (50 ft)

Weight:

453,6 kg (1000 lbs)

Fire Type:

Fusing blasts

Weaknesses:

  • Sunlight sensitivity

Trivia:

  • Possible that the Sandbuster's ability to swim through sand is based on that of the sandfish, a type of skink that lives in the Sahara.
  • One of the few dragons to be sensitive to light, the first being the Whispering Death .
  • The Sandbuster's treasure hoarding and protectiveness of its lair is a reference to dragon behavior in many real-world myths and stories.
