Buffalord

× >

Mystery Class

First appearance:

Race to the Edge, Season 3, Episode 5 "Buffalord Soldier"

All appearances:

Race to the Edge, Season 3, Episode 5 "Buffalord Soldier"

Behaviour and personality:

Buffalords do not leave their homelands because of their diet. They can quickly become enraged and highly aggressive until order in its life is finally restored, allowing them to calm again to their docile temperament. A major case for such behaviour can be leading them too far away from its territory and food source, as it will do anything in its power to return to its home and instantly return to calmly grazing.

Features:

  • Armoured body
  • Ram-like horns
  • Small rows of spines along back
  • Short legs

Abilities:

  • Inflating its body to an immense size
  • Shooting spines from any part of its body
  • Extremely potent saliva
  • Saliva is the only cure to the Scourge of Odin

Weaknesses:

  • Docility. Leading to them being hunted to extinct when there was a big outbreak of the Scourge of Odin.
  • High-pitched sounds. Can induce enraged behaviour.

Trivia:

  • Its flames is not hot enough to melt metal.
  • Name is derived from "Buffalo", a common alternative name for the American Bison. The Bison and Buffalord share behaviours.
  • Appears to be the first confirmed herbivorous dragon.
