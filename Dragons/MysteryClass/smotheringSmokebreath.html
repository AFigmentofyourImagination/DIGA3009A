Smothering Smokebreath

< × >

Mystery Class

First appearance:

All appearances:

Behaviour and personality:

Smokebreaths create nests from metal that they weld together. They live in large flights and work together, and thus they travel together in a cloud of smoke. It is because they prefer to stay hidden and aren't aggressive, though they are territorial and will defend their homes.

Trivia:

Similar to both a bat and a magpie. Similar in behaviour and stature to Terrible Terrors.

It is possible that Smothering Smokebreaths used to gather raw ore to make their nests. With the arrival of Vikings to the archipelago 300 years earlier, they may have then adapted their behaviour to also scavenge Viking metal as it would be easier to collect.

Smothering Smokebreaths and Armorwings appear to be natural enemies, as they fight over and steal scrap metal.
