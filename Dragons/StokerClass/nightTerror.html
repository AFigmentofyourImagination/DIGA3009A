Behaviour and personality:

Night Terrors are nocturnal dragons that live as a colony. They also have a great respect for their Alpha and have shown to be playful and slightly dog-like.

Features:

  • Double nasal horn
  • Horn under chin
  • Short fattish tail with four or more short, thin spikes on it

Abilities:

  • Creates massive flocks to appear as a larger dragon to scare off predators

Diet:

Unknown / Never mentioned / Presumably fish

Habitat:

Forests

Caves

Size:

0,91 m (3 ft)

Wingspan:

1,52 m (5 ft)

Weight:

6,35 kg (14 lbs)

Fire Type:

Several discreet fire streams from each dragon coalesce into one giant fireball when in flock formation

Weaknesses:

  • Flight (group) and leader dependency

Trivia:

  • Originally, Tuffnut wanted to call the dragons "Smidvarg and the Gang".
  • The Red Death's roar was reused in one of the Night Terrors' roars.
  • Both black and white Night Terrors behave very similarly to bats.
  • The Night Terror's tail is quite similar to that of a coelacanth.
  • The design of the Night Terror's head (not counting the horns) resembles the head of a parrot.
