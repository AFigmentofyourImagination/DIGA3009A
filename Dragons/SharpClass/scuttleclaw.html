Behaviour and personality:

As juveniles, Scuttleclaws are mischievous and playful and listen to no one. Not even a Bewilderbeast can put them under command.

Juveniles tend to gnaw on anything they can find, most likely due to their stage of teething. They are described to be "hyperactive powerhouses". This trait could be, rather eccentrically, one of few countermeasures effective to resist the hypnosis of alpha dragons.

Scuttleclaws often scatter or become disciplined when they are roared at by larger and more mature dragons.
