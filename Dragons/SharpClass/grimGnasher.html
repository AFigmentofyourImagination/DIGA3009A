Grim Gnasher

< × >

Sharp Class

First appearance:

All appearances:

Behaviour and personality:

Grim Gnashers are scheming and cunning predators that tend to plot against their prey. They exploit the weaknesses of the old and sick dragons on Vanaheim, a dragon cemetery for dragons to spend the last of their days.

While the Grim Gnashers are villainized, in a functioning ecosystem, these dragons are an important part of removing weak animals and, presumably, cleaning up carrion, allowing the food cycle to continue, just as real-life scavengers do.

Features:

  • Snake-like body
  • Lots of horns and spikes
  • Shark like-teeth

Abilities:

  • Immunity to Slitherwing venom

Trivia:

  • The Grim Gnashers' ability to shoot their teeth is possibly inspired by those of the Poison Darters or Razorwings from the "How to Train Your Dragon" Book series.
  • Possible to be natural enemies with Sentinels.
  • The word Gnasher means teeth, which is fitting since they use their teeth as weapons.
  • The Grim Gnasher's design resembles Cynder from "The Legend of Spyro" and "Skylanders".
