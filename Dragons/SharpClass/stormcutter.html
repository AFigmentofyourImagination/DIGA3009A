Behaviour and personality:

Stormcutters, much like the Night Fury and Speed Stinger, can hang themselves upside-down from higher ground, similar to a bat. Their resting position gives them a slightly peculiar and mysterious atmosphere.

They seem to keep to themselves most of the time, preferring not to interact with other dragon species.
