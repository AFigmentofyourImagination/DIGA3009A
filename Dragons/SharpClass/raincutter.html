Raincutter

× >

Sharp Class

First appearance:

How to Train Your Dragon 2

All appearances:

How to Train Your Dragon 2

How to Train Your Dragon 3: The Hidden World

Behaviour and personality:

Raincutters love wet, rainy places. The rainy weather helps bring up the usually soil-bound worms and grubs that act as the Raincutter's main food source. Like the Scauldron , these dragons dislike hot weather and both species appear to be fairly similar. However, it is unknown if the Raincutter lives in the water. They seem to be very comfortable with the cold.

Features:

  • Large sharp fins on the back and head
  • Nasal horn
  • Elongated neck
  • Short hooked tendrils under the chin
  • Larger lower jaw
  • Aerodynamic fins used for flight and offense

Abilities:

  • Picking up food with its long neck
  • Rain-resistant firepower
