Behaviour and personality:

Rumblehorns are highly intelligent and determined. They always have their snouts to the ground, sniffing around for anything edible. They also have a keen sense of smell and can track the scent of a human or other dragon. They can often be aggressive but are friendly towards their riders.

They run and charge like rhinoceroses. Their food of choice is unknown, but it is known that they are carnivorous. Rumblehorns can be very caring and protective of those who are in danger, as seen when Skullcrusher tried to warn everyone of a tidal wave heading towards Dragon's Edge.

Rumblehorns appear to be solitary and elusive creatures that are rarely seen due to their stealth. As shown by Skullcrusher in "Last Auction Heroes", sometimes when Rumblehorns are getting ready to sleep, they singe the ground with their fiery breath while walking in a circle, much like Night Furies do before lying down.

Trivia:

  • The Rumblehorn bears a mild resemblance to the Eight-Legged Battlegore from the books.
  • As seen in How to Train Your Dragon: The Hidden World, the Rumblehorn is shown to be able to move independently and rattle the armoured plates on its neck region. Rumblehorns were also shown to be able to stand and walk on two legs in the film, but prefer to walk or run on all fours, much like a bear.
  • Rumblehorns made an appearance in the trailer for Minecraft's How to Train Your Dragon DLC, and have been put into the DLC as a blocky, tameable, rideable dragon.
