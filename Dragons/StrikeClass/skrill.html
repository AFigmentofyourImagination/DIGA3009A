Behaviour and personality:

The Skrill, listed as one of the most enigmatic and feared species in the Dragon Manual, possesses traits that make it both aggressive and exceptionally powerful, rendering it nearly impossible to train. According to Ryker, Skrills are exceedingly rare and hold a reputation similar to the feared Night Fury, owing to their historical association with the Berserkers who harnessed them as weapons.

This reclusive dragon exhibits belligerent behaviour and unpredictability akin to lightning strikes. Despite its affinity for stormy weather, it displays a strong territorial nature. Skrills are renowned for their elusiveness and inherent distrust towards other dragon species as well as humans. Seeking lightning storms, they utilize such environments to defend themselves against potential predators or to rapidly recharge their limited projectile capacity.

Their ferocity is well-known, often leading them to act impulsively when released from prolonged entrapment in ice. Furthermore, Skrills possess an intense capacity for vengeance, relentlessly pursuing those they perceive as having wronged them. This was exemplified by the Frozen Skrill, which sought retribution against Hiccup and Toothless for re-imprisoning it in the glacier.
