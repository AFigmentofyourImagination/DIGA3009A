Whispering Death

First appearance:

All appearances:

Behaviour and personality:

When their teeth move, they make an eerie noise that sounds like whispering, hence their name. The noise along with the ground rumbling are the only warnings you get that a Whispering Death is near. These dragons remember things very well, resulting in them recognizing people who have helped them in the past and also carry grudges.

They are extremely vicious, yet enjoys having their teeth brushed. When finding intruders in their tunnels, they eject them from the nearest pit. They are more dangerous in their infant stages because they have less control over their teeth and spines.
