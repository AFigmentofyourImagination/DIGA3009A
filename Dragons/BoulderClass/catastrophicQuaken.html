Behaviour and personality:

The Catastrophic Quaken can smash into the ground and create huge shockwaves that can knock dragons out of air.

When threatened, its immediate reaction is to enter protective mode and roll into a ball like a giant armadillo. Once secured into this position, it quickly takes the offense, smashing the ground to create shockwaves so powerful they will knock other dragons down whether they are on land or airborne.

Often described in texts as the "bully of the dragon world" due to their extreme territorial and solitary nature. Ironically, they have a tough exterior before you can witness how loyal and friendly they can be.

Trivia:

  • Relies on size as ultimate defence mechanism.
  • This dragon used to be known as the Catastrophic Quake; however, for some reason, an extra "n" was added to the end of this dragon's name. This may have been to make the dragon's name sound like "Kraken".
  • The Catastrophic Quaken was named by Fishlegs, but it was often referred to as just the Quaken.
  • The Catastrophic Quaken, as a species, was confirmed to have existed way before production on "Race to the Edge" even started. Its design was based off of Nico Marlet's concept art for Skullcrusher in "How to Train Your Dragon 2".
  • Fishlegs states that Bork's Papers mention Dark Deep, but it is in context to a different species than the Gronckle (this could be referring to the Catastrophic Quaken).
