Gronckle

First appearance:

All appearances:

Features in every media

Behaviour and personality:

Lazy and spend most time in heaps while sleeping, like a puppy pile. Have fallen asleep while flying and only waking up when they crash into something. They are real softies despite their bodily exterior, like a giant heavy puppy that can crush you. They don't enjoy combat and will only fight for defence or territorial purposes. They are very social creatures and enjoy physical contact.

Gronckles are very intelligent and fast learners, kind of like dogs, as they can learn tricks and learn defence from other dragons. And, like dogs, they whine when upset.

Trivia:

Design is inspired by a mix of a pit-bull, a crocodile, a Harley-Davidson, a bumblebee (stature), a helicopter (movement), walruses & rhinos (behaviour and movements), hummingbird (wings).

Because of the wing-to-body ratio, their wings would have to beat approximately 4,1 times faster than a hummingbird.

Their wings move back and forth like a bumblebee.

They originally came from Dark Deep.

It is likely that they carry their offspring in their large jaws like crocodiles.
