Behaviour and personality:

They are inquisitive and playful, making them some of the friendliest dragons ever. And are incredibly trusting to the point that dragon trappers can easily hunt them. These dragons are extremely active and playful among the usual Boulder class behaviour. Their tendency to stay in places where crystals are abundant suggests that they can sniff it out.
