Behaviour and personality:

Real lazy dragon, they move slowly in a lizard-like fashion, known to be clumsy, and often land with a flop on the ground because they just guess when and where to land.

Combat is a different tale, though. Then this dragon goes into a frenzy when provoked. Their personality is that of a lazy cat: high appetite, but mostly eat scraps.

Features:

  • Eyes lower on head
  • Clubbed tail

Abilities:

  • Sleep-flying
  • Immense jaw strength that can bite through Dragon Proof Metal

Diet:

Almost anything, really

Trivia:

  • Since Hotburples are very similar to Gronckles in terms of appearance, behavior, and abilities, it is likely that the two dragon species are from the same family, just like the Night Fury and Light Fury.
  • Designed to look similar to a Gronckle, but with elements of inspiration from a walrus, tadpole, bulldog and rubbish bin.
