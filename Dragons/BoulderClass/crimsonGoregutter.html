Crimson Goregutter

× >

Boulder Class

First appearance:

How to Train Your Dragon 3: The Hidden World

All appearances:

How to Train Your Dragon 3: The Hidden World

Behaviour and personality:

Gentler than they appear and like to be left in peace. If in battle, it has the ability to spew molten lava onto their antlers before ramming opponents like a bull. Loyal to their Alpha and caring to friends.

Features:

  • Metallic, moose-like antlers
  • Axe-shaped tail
  • Rock-shaped spines
  • Blazing antlers

Abilities:

  • Ear-splitting call

Trivia:

  • The Crimson Goregutter's ability to produce ear splitting calls is likely inspired by the calls of elk during the mating season, as elks are closely related to moose, the animal that is the main basis for this dragon species.
  • They are intended to look prehistoric and imposing yet dosile.
  • Their antlers were inspired by an extinct species of elk, the Megaloceros.
