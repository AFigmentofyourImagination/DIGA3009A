Shovelhelm

First appearance:

How to train your Dragon 2

All appearances:

How to train your Dragon 2

How to train your Dragon 3

Behaviour and personality:

These are sociable dragons with a friendly temperament to other dragons, and love to fix and build things. They're known to be level-headed and intelligent, they like to examine the situation first, then evaluate it, and solve it. This is probably why other dragons love them. Shovelhelms are the stable friend who listens to your problems and help you when you need to move.

Trivia:

The Shovelhelm was created using a modular system to mix and match preset body parts onto different dragons. It was created from the same base that was used for the Snafflefang, Snifflehunch, Thunderclaw, Windgnasher, and two other Unknown Dragons.
